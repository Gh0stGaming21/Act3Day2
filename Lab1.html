About Me

Hi, my name is Clavio Sean Zebedee A., and I am 19 years old. I was born on the 3rd of October 2005. As the eldest of the grandchildren in our family, I carry both a sense of pride and responsibility that comes with being the firstborn of the younger generation. My life has been a mixture of challenges and triumphs, shaped by personal experiences that have molded me into the person I am today.

Life Challenges

One of the pivotal events of my life occurred when I was very young. My father abandoned me and my mother, choosing instead to be with another family. This abandonment was a difficult reality to process, especially during my formative years. However, it also taught me the importance of resilience and finding strength within myself. Despite this, my mother’s unwavering love and support have been my anchor. She worked tirelessly to ensure I had a stable upbringing, even when she had to make sacrifices.

Hobbies

This curiosity fuels my passion for growth and adventure.

Education

Currently, I am pursuing a degree in Information Technology at Lorma Colleges. Choosing this field of study stems from my fascination with technology and its ever-evolving nature. I believe that technology has the power to shape the future, and I want to be part of that change. My studies have not only deepened my understanding of IT but have also ignited a desire to contribute meaningfully to the industry. I envision myself developing innovative solutions and creating a positive impact through technology.

Memorable Moments

One of the most memorable moments in my life was during my elementary school graduation. This event holds a special place in my heart because it marked a significant milestone in my academic journey and brought my family together in a way that I will never forget. At the time, my mother had been working abroad to provide for us. While she was away, I was cared for by my grandparents, aunts, and uncles. Their love and guidance were instrumental in shaping me during her absence.

More About Me

This trip not only strengthened my bond with my family but also left me with memories that I will cherish forever.
